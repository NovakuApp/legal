1. Introduction

Welcome to NOVAKU: History ("the App"), an Android application developed by Jon Ander Moraleda ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Jon Ander Moraleda ("we", "our", or "us") acts as the Data Controller for personal data processed through NOVAKU: History, as defined under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

We are committed to protecting your personal information. Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the practices described herein. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App is designed to function without requiring you to create an account. However, we collect certain information depending on how you choose to use the App:

2.1 Account Information (Optional)

Account creation is entirely voluntary. If you choose to register, we collect:

  • Name — displayed in your profile within the App.
  • Email address — used to identify your account and enable cross-device sync.

If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account's name, email address, and profile photo URL as provided by Google Sign-In.

2.2 Learning Progress and Activity Data

To provide the core learning experience, the App records:

  • Quiz scores, number of attempts, and incorrect answers per lesson.
  • Total XP (experience points) earned and study streak (consecutive days of study).
  • Number of lessons completed.
  • Badges and achievements unlocked.

This data is stored locally on your device and, if you have an account, also synced to our cloud database.

2.3 App Preferences

We store your in-app settings locally, including:

  • Preferred language (English or Spanish).
  • Daily study goal.
  • Premium subscription status.

2.4 Advertising Identifier (AdMob)

For users on the free tier, the App displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID) to serve personalized or contextual advertisements. You can reset or opt out of ad personalization in your device's settings under Google > Ads. Premium subscribers do not see ads.

2.5 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect precise geolocation, camera data, microphone data, contacts, SMS, call logs, or any other sensitive device data. The only Android permission the App requests is INTERNET.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • Providing the service: To deliver lessons, track quiz progress, calculate XP and streaks, and unlock badges.
  • Syncing your progress: If you have an account, to synchronize your learning data across devices via Supabase.
  • Displaying advertisements: To show relevant ads to free-tier users through Google AdMob, thereby keeping the App free.
  • Managing subscriptions: To verify and restore your premium subscription status via RevenueCat and Google Play Billing.
  • Authentication: To securely identify your account when using Google Sign-In.
  • Improving the App: Aggregated, anonymized usage patterns may be used to improve the learning experience and content quality.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each with its own privacy practices:

4.1 Supabase

We use Supabase as our backend database to store user accounts and synced learning progress. Supabase servers are located in the EU (Ireland / AWS eu-west-1) region, ensuring your data remains within the European Union. Supabase complies with GDPR. For more information, see the Supabase Privacy Policy.

4.2 Google Sign-In

If you choose to authenticate with your Google account, Google processes your authentication data. This is an optional feature; you may use the App anonymously or with a local account. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4.3 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions (History Pro, NOVAKU Pro). RevenueCat may process your purchase history and device identifiers to verify subscription status and handle restores. For more information, see the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

4.4 Google AdMob

Free-tier users are shown advertisements served by Google AdMob, a service by Google LLC. AdMob may collect and use the Android Advertising ID and other signals to deliver personalized or contextual ads. Premium subscribers do not receive ads and AdMob data collection does not apply to them. For more information, see Google's Advertising Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

  • Local storage: Data stored on your Android device is protected by the device's built-in security (AES-256 encryption on supported devices via Android Keystore).
  • Cloud storage: User data synced to Supabase is stored in encrypted databases hosted in the EU (Ireland). Supabase uses TLS 1.2+ for all data in transit.
  • Preferences: App preferences are stored using Android's DataStore API, which is private to the App and inaccessible to other apps.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

6.1 Access and Portability

You can view all your learning progress, statistics, and preferences directly within the App's profile and progress screens.

6.2 Deletion

You may delete your data in the following ways:

  • Local progress: Use the "Reset all progress" option in the Profile screen to erase all locally stored progress, XP, streaks, and badges.
  • Account data: Signing out of your account removes your email address, avatar, and Supabase account ID from the device. To request permanent deletion of your cloud data, contact us at jonander.moraleda@oroi.eu.

When you delete your account or reset your progress, your data is removed from your device immediately. Data stored on our servers (Supabase) is permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.

6.3 Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your preferences in your Android device settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization. Alternatively, purchasing a premium subscription removes all ads entirely.

6.4 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, rectify, restrict processing, object to processing, and request erasure of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at jonander.moraleda@oroi.eu.

7. Children's Privacy

NOVAKU: History is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at jonander.moraleda@oroi.eu so that we can delete it.

Users between the ages of 13 and 16 should have parental consent before registering an account, in accordance with applicable local laws (e.g., GDPR Article 8 in EU member states).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. For material changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users within the App.

Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
