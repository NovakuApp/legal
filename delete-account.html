Delete Your NOVAKU Account

To delete your account and associated data from NOVAKU: History, follow these steps:

  1. Open the NOVAKU: History app on your device.
  2. Go to Profile (bottom navigation bar, last icon).
  3. Tap Reset Progress to delete all local and cloud progress data.
  4. Tap Sign Out to disconnect your account.

This will permanently delete:

  • All lesson progress, quiz scores, and XP
  • All unlocked badges and achievements
  • Your streak and stats
  • Your account data stored on our servers

Your display name and language preference stored locally on your device will be cleared when you uninstall the app.
